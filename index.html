About This App 🔐

This web application is a secure and user-friendly tool developed by Irish Diane Lee Iniego. Here's what it offers:

  • 🔍Password Strength Checker: Evaluates your password based on key factors like length, character variety, and repetition. Provides real-time feedback on its strength.
  • ⚙️Password Generator: Generates strong, random passwords using a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. Adjust the length to meet your security needs.
  • 🌗Theme Support: Toggle between light and dark modes for a more personalized and comfortable user experience.
  • 🔒Privacy Focused: All password evaluations and generations happen locally in your browser, ensuring that your data stays private and secure.
